The North Pole weather widget tells Santa whether it is safe to fly. It works at normal zoom, but when users increase the text size, some of the content becomes hidden.

Your mission today

Check that people can increase text size to at least 200% without losing content or functionality.

Quick 5-minute check

  • open a page in your service
  • use the browser zoom or system text size settings to increase text to around 200%
  • check that all content is still visible and readable
  • make sure important controls are not pushed off-screen or overlapped

Test the North Pole weather widget

Below is a small widget. It uses a fixed height, which can cause problems when text size is increased.

North Pole weather - Christmas Eve

Visibility: excellent
Wind: light tailwind
Temperature: very cold, but manageable
Backup route: available through Lapland air corridor

Increase the browser zoom or text size. Some of the text may become clipped or require awkward scrolling.

The WCAG 2.2 skill behind this

1.4.4 resize text - users must be able to resize text up to 200% without loss of content or functionality.

Why this matters

Many people increase text size to read comfortably. Fixed-height containers can cause important information to disappear or become hard to reach.

Bonus challenge

Try removing the fixed height from the widget in dev tools. How does it behave when zoomed in?
