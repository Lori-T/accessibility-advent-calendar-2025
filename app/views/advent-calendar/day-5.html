Day 5: Check keyboard access on the Gift Tracker

The Department of Elfish Affairs has received a report that the Gift Tracker, the system elves use to update toy progress, cannot be fully used without a mouse.

Some controls appear interactive but cannot be reached or operated using a keyboard, creating a barrier for users who rely on keyboard navigation.

Your mission today

Check that all interactive controls can be accessed and used with a keyboard.

Quick 5-minute check

  • use the Tab key to move forward through controls
  • use Shift + Tab to move backwards
  • buttons, links and form fields must all receive focus
  • interactive elements must be usable without a mouse
