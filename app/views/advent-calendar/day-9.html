Day 9: Check reflow on the Elf Equipment Dashboard

The Elf Equipment Dashboard shows the status of tools and machinery used across Santa HQ. Today, you're checking how well this layout adapts when users use screen magnification or zoom in.

Two of these dashboard tiles reflow properly. One has a deliberate reflow failure - and will break the layout when zoomed.

Your mission today

Zoom the page between 200% and 400% and observe what happens when reflow isn't properly implemented.

Dashboard tiles

✔ Wrapping machine

This tile reflows correctly.

Online
❌ Ribbon cutter

This tile has a fixed oversized width and will not reflow properly.

Offline
✔ Sleigh polish station

This tile reflows correctly.

Maintenance

What this checks for

1.4.10 Reflow -content must adapt at narrow widths and high zoom without forcing horizontal scrolling.

Why this matters

Many users zoom pages to read comfortably. If layouts do not adapt, information becomes hidden or unusable.

Bonus challenge

Compare the dashboard at 100%, 200%, 300% and 400% on your device and observe how it behaves. You could also try it on a mobile phone or tablet.
