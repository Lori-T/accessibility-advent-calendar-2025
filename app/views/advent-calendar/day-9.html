Users who need larger text often zoom in significantly. Your page must remain usable and readable when zoomed to 400%.

What you’re checking today

You’re checking that your layout responds appropriately when zoomed, without causing horizontal scrolling or clipping.

Quick 5-minute check

  • use your browser’s zoom controls to increase zoom to 400%
  • check that your content rearranges into a single column
  • ensure all text remains readable and nothing disappears off-screen
  • test menus, navigation, forms and buttons
  • confirm there is no horizontal scrolling

The WCAG 2.2 skill behind this

1.4.10 Reflow - content must reflow without forcing horizontal scrolling at up to 400% zoom.

Why this matters

Reflow supports people with low vision, cognitive disabilities and users on small screens.

Try this bonus check

Rotate your phone to portrait mode. Does it behave similarly to 400% zoom?
