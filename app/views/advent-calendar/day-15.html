Captions ensure that videos are accessible to Deaf users and anyone who cannot listen to audio. Captions should be accurate, complete and synchronised.

What you're checking today

You're checking whether any pre-recorded videos on your site have correct captions.

Quick 5-minute check

  • play the video and look for a CC or Subtitles button
  • turn the captions on
  • check that speech, sound effects and important audio cues are included
  • look for synchronisation problems
  • if captions are missing, check the HTML for <track kind="captions">

The WCAG 2.2 skill behind this

1.2.2 Captions (Prerecorded) - all prerecorded videos must have captions.

Why this matters

Captions support Deaf users, people in noisy environments and those who process information better through text than audio.

Try this bonus check

Turn off the sound completely. Can you still understand the video?
