Day 9: Check reflow on the Elf Equipment Dashboard

The Elf Equipment Dashboard shows the status of tools and machinery used across Santa HQ. When elves zoom in to make text easier to read, some dashboard panels refuse to resize - forcing sideways scrolling.

Today you'll check whether these cards reflow properly when zoomed, and whether the layout adapts without hiding content.

Your mission today

Make sure content is usable at between 200% and 400% zoom without horizontal scrolling.

Quick 5-minute check

  • zoom the page to between 200% and 400%
  • content should reflow into a single column
  • there should be no horizontal scrolling
  • avoid fixed widths that stop elements resizing

Test the dashboard

One of the equipment cards below has a deliberate reflow issue. When zoomed, it will not resize with the rest of the content.

What this checks for

1.4.10 reflow - content must work without horizontal scrolling, even at narrow widths or high zoom levels.

Why this matters

Many people zoom pages to make text readable. If layouts do not reflow, users can lose content off-screen or be forced to scroll sideways — making tasks much harder.

Bonus challenge
