Accessibility is not a one-time activity - it's a habit. Small checks performed regularly help prevent issues from building up and create more inclusive digital experiences.

What you're checking today

You're building a habit of doing a quick accessibility check every time you work on digital content.

Quick 5-minute practice

Choose one quick check each day, such as:

  • colour contrast
  • headings and structure
  • keyboard navigation
  • focus styles
  • link text clarity
  • alt text
  • form labels
  • responsive behaviour or zoom

The WCAG 2.2 skill behind this

This brings together everything you have learned through the advent calendar - a simple daily habit that keeps accessibility front of mind.

Why this matters

Regular, small checks help teams catch issues early, reduce accessibility debt and build confidence in creating accessible content and services.

Try this bonus check

Add a 10-minute weekly accessibility sweep to your team's routine. It makes a big difference over time.
