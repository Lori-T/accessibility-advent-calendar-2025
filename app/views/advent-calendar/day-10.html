Day 10: Check captions on Santa’s video message

Every year, Santa records a short video message for the elves. But this year’s message has been uploaded without captions or any form of transcript.

Today’s task is to check whether the video provides accessible alternatives for people who cannot hear the audio.

Your mission today

Check whether the video includes captions or a transcript.

Quick 5-minute check

  • videos with audio must have captions
  • captions should include speech and meaningful sounds
  • provide a transcript if captions are not available
  • auto-generated captions often contain errors

Test Santa’s video

This video contains a deliberate accessibility issue.

❌ Accessibility issue: no captions or transcript provided.

What this checks for

1.2.2 captions (prerecorded) - videos must provide captions.

Why this matters

Without captions, people who are D/deaf or hard of hearing miss important content.

Bonus challenge

Turn off your sound and watch the video. How much can you understand without captions?
