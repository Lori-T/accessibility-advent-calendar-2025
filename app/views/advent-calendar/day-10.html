When users navigate using the keyboard, they need a clear visual indicator showing where they are on the page. This indicator is called the focus outline.

What you’re checking today

You’re checking whether the focus outline is clear, visible and high contrast on every interactive element.

Quick 5-minute check

  • press Tab repeatedly to move through the page
  • check that a clear focus indicator appears on every interactive element
  • ensure the indicator is high contrast and visible in all environments
  • check custom components (like styled buttons) which often lose focus styles

The WCAG 2.2 skill behind this

2.4.7 Focus Visible - keyboard focus must always be visible.

Why this matters

Without a visible focus indicator, keyboard users become lost and unable to complete tasks.

Try this bonus check

Try tabbing around in bright light or outdoors. Can you still see the focus outline clearly?
